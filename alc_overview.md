# Adaptive Learning Coach – KB

**What is ALC?**  
The Adaptive Learning Coach personalizes micro-lessons based on a baseline quiz and short pulse surveys, with privacy by design (n ≥ 5 aggregation).

**Motivation & Theory**  
Grounded in self-efficacy (Bandura), learning goal orientation (Dweck), and self-determination theory (Deci & Ryan).

**Data Model**  
Entities: users (alias), survey_responses, lessons, lesson_events, chat_logs. Retention: 90 days for raw events.
